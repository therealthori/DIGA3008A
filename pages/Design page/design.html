Main Goals

  • Increase Usability: In a way that the product is easy to use, intuitive, and efficient for the users to perform their tasks.
  • Promote Consistency: To maintain a coherent structure, layout, and interaction patterns across the product, reducing learning time.
  • Encourage Learnability: To design interfaces that users can quickly understand and master, even on first use.
  • Facilitate Feedback and Response: To provide timely and meaningful feedback that helps users understand system status and results of actions.
